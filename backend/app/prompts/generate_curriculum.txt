LEVEL LOAD — do not deviate from these ranges for the "{level}" level:
- beginner:     10–12 hours/week, 3 focus items, 2 deliverables, 6 resources, 3–4 checklist items
- intermediate: 14–16 hours/week, 4 focus items, 2–3 deliverables, 6 resources, 5 checklist items
- advanced:     18–22 hours/week, 5 focus items, 3 deliverables, 6 resources, 6 checklist items
Every week MUST have exactly 6 resources (see RESOURCE MIX below — 3 video + 3 non-video). A 3-month beginner plan MUST NOT have the same weekly load as a 3-month advanced plan.

BLOOM'S TAXONOMY — progressive cognitive levels by month position:
