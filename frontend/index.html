About AI Learning Roadmap

✕

What is this?
A free, structured study plan that takes you from Python fundamentals to deployed AI applications. No paywalls, no ads, no fluff — just a clear path with curated resources from top universities and practitioners.

Who is it for?
Working developers, CS students, and career changers who want a serious, accountable roadmap to break into AI/ML engineering.

How it works

1.Pick a plan — 3, 6, or 12 months based on your pace 2.Follow week by week — each week has focus areas, deliverables, and curated resources 3.Check off tasks — progress syncs across devices when signed in 4.Link your GitHub repos — connect your work to each week 5.Get AI evaluation — AI reviews your repos and gives feedback 6.Ask the AI Mentor — get help on any topic in your current week 7.Share milestones — post achievements to LinkedIn
